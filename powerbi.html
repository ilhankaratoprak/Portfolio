HR Analytics Dashboard

The HR Analytics Dashboard is designed to provide insightful metrics about the workforce within an organization. It enables HR professionals to monitor employee dynamics, attrition rates, and job satisfaction levels effectively.

  • Employee Metrics: Displays the total number of employees and active workforce, allowing for quick assessments of workforce size and engagement.
  • Attrition Analysis: Offers a comprehensive view of employee turnover, segmented by departments and age groups. This helps identify areas with higher attrition rates and potential underlying issues.
  • Job Satisfaction Ratings: : Highlights employee satisfaction across different roles, providing valuable insights for improving workplace culture and retention strategies.
  • Demographic Insights: Analyses the workforce based on age and gender, aiding in understanding diversity and inclusivity within the organization.
