Wealth of Nations Analysis

This project presents a comprehensive approach to visualizing and analyzing economic data from "The Wealth of Nations" using both Excel and Tableau. It focuses on the following key areas:

  • Data Management Policies: A discussion on the essential policies that govern data security, confidentiality, and ethical use, ensuring compliance with GDPR and best practices in data protection.
  • Excel Data Visualization:Offers a comprehensive view of employee turnover, segmented by departments and age groups. This helps identify areas with higher attrition rates and potential underlying issues.
  • Tableau Visualizations: The final part explores advanced data visualizations using Tableau.

This project provides valuable insights into the relationship between economic indicators and their visual representation, demonstrating key technical skills in both Excel and Tableau
