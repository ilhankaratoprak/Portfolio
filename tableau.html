Car Sales Report

This dashboard is designed to provide insights into the current year's (CY) car sales performance. It includes the following key analyses:

  • Weekly Sales Trend: Shows the week-by-week changes in car sales, highlighting seasonal patterns and trends over time.
  • CY Total Sales by Body Style: Displays the total number of cars sold, broken down by body style, including Saloon, SUV, Hatchback, and others.
  • CY Total Sales by Colour: Analyses the distribution of car sales based on colour preferences, highlighting the most popular colours among buyers.
  • CY Cars Sold by Dealer Region: Breaks down car sales by different regions and dealerships, identifying areas with higher sales activity.
  • Company-wise Sales Trend: Compares the sales performance of different car manufacturers over time.

PIZZA SALES

×

Pizza Sales Report

This Pizza Sales Report Dashboard provides a detailed analysis of pizza sales performance from 1st January 2015 to 31st December 2015. It presents insights into total revenue, average order value, total pizzas sold, and sales trends based on pizza categories and sizes. The dashboard aims to support decision-makers in understanding customer preferences, identifying the best and least-selling pizzas, and highlighting opportunities for growth based on time-based trends. Key features of the dashboard include:

  • Revenue Summary: Displays total revenue for the year and the contribution of different pizza categories and sizes.
  • Top and Bottom Performers: Lists the best and least-selling pizzas by revenue, quantity sold, and total orders
  • Time-based Trends:Highlights peak sales hours, busiest days, and weeks to help optimise operations during high-demand periods.
  • Sales by Pizza Category and Size:Breaks down sales by different pizza types (e.g., Veggie, Chicken, Classic) and sizes (e.g., Large, Medium, Regular), helping to identify top-performing product categories.

This dashboard is designed to deliver actionable insights and assist in optimising inventory, menu choices, and promotional strategies to drive higher sales and customer satisfaction.

ROAD ACCIDENT REPORT

×

Road Accident Project


This Road Accident Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of road accident data. The dashboard aims to deliver in-depth insights into the frequency and severity of road accidents, thereby assisting in the development of safety strategies. It encompasses key metrics such as total accidents, casualty figures, accident severity, weather conditions, and the impact of road surfaces.


This analysis showcases the contributions of different vehicle types to accidents, as well as the distribution of slight and serious casualties. Furthermore, it evaluates the role of weather conditions (such as rain, snow, and fog) in the occurrence of accidents.


The dashboard enables users to:

  • Accident Trends: Understand the changes in accident numbers over time, providing insights into trends and patterns.
  • Casualty Figures: Analyse the number of casualties resulting from accidents, highlighting the need for safety measures.
  • Analysis by Road Surface and Weather Conditions: Examine the impact of various road surfaces and weather conditions on accident occurrences, facilitating the identification of strategies to enhance safety.

    • This dashboard aims to provide valuable insights for road safety professionals and policymakers, helping them make informed decisions to reduce accidents and improve road safety.

The Wealth of Nations Report

This report provides a comprehensive analysis aimed at understanding the economic welfare levels and quality of life in various countries. Data on life expectancy at birth, GDP per capita, and technology usage are employed to examine the wealth and social dynamics of nations.


Key insights include high life expectancy, advanced healthcare systems, and significant indicators of economic performance. Furthermore, the increase in digitalisation and access to technology is directly linked to the economic growth of countries.


This dashboard serves as a valuable resource for researchers and policymakers, offering insights that assist in the development of economic strategies.
